Six Of Crows Duology by Leigh Bardugo

01 April 2026   ★★★★★

Mode: Physical copy, paper-back.

Thoughts: Childhood favorites, both books(Six Of Crows and Crooked Kingdom)! Re-reading them for the 3rd time, largely because i forgot the plot and remembered loving the characters deeply. It is very fast paced for my hopelessly short attention span, the found family element is my favorite thing in any book, and this one does it well.

Fantasy gets unfairly dismissed sometimes, but these are the ones that make me truly love reading. The book has flaws, the characters behave like they're way beyond their stated age, but this is my favorite book nonetheless, the heist, the planning, the fights, the money. Absolute page turner, highly recommended always!
